Note: All of the following (often short-lived) journals and trade papers are available on Microfilm at UC Berkeley or through Interlibrary Loan. In the past decade, more journals have become available on microfilm, and a growing number of them can now be at least partially accessed through the digitial library. For links, see Film Journals Available Online on this site. For a comprehensive online library of mostly American film and media journals, click here.
